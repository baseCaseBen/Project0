Letterboxing

What is it?

Letterboxing is an activity similar to geocatching. Letterboxing uses a list of instructions and a map to find a hidden box containing a notebook and a rubber stamp. Each letterboxer carves his or her own stamp to exchange with each find.

Where are they?

There are letterboxes hidden all around the world, making it very easy to get started

Atlas Quest is the most popular database of letterboxes
